Fashion of the Future

is a project aiming to showcase the potential of biotextile use in fashion. As the world increasingly seeks sustainable and eco-friendly alternatives to combat pollution and the harmful effects of fast fashion, biotextiles have emerged as a promising solution. By integrating biotextiles, which are derived from natural and renewable sources, the 6 pieces highlighted serve to reduce negative environmental impacts and open the doors for a more sustainable future for the fashion industry.
